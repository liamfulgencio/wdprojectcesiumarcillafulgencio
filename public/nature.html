DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: One With Nature

Embody the serenity of the great outdoors with the "One With Nature" theme! This theme focuses on outfits that, by what the name suggests, focuses on nature-inspired looks. Channel the essence of forests, oceans, or skies through your creations.


Inspired by earthy tones, organic textures, and natural beauty, the theme focuses as well on the beauty of the environment. You can go for looks similar to those usually seen in nature like flowers, trees, waters, and others. You can try focusing on shades of green, brown, beige, blue, and neutral tones. If not, you can go for colorful tones as long as you're still going for that nature look. Avoid bold patterns and bright colors. Try going for subtle earthy tones
