DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Carnival

The Carnival theme mainly focuses on vibrant culture and self-expression. This theme also goes for a colorful and extravagant style while sticking to a party-like theme.


Some tips for this theme is to go for bright, bold hues of colors like red, yellow, and the like. You may also go for decorative accessories like headdresses and capes. Try wearing things as well like masks and elaborate hats. You can also try cosplaying the usual people seen in carnivals like jesters, bears, vendors, or ringmasters.
