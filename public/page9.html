DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Visual Kei

Visual Kei is a Japanese-inspired theme. It emphasizes dramatic fashion and aesthetic expression. It is mainly characterized by heavy makeup, elaborate hairstyles, and flamboyant clothing. It also aims to spark creativity and artistry.

Some tips you can use for this theme is going for big, bold hairstyles. Go for metallic accents along with ruffles. With it being influenced by rock, punk, and gothic styles, you can also try going for heavy eyeliner and dramatic styling.
