DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Dripping in Gold

As the name suggests, this theme centers around the use of gold in your outfits. It is inspired by luxury and wealth and uses features like gold accents with a high-end style. Try going for glamorous and extravagant outfits.


The main tips that can be given for this theme is to mainly use gold hues and nothing else. Try aiming for a rich, gold look and avoid going for dull shades. You can also use the foil material to make the gold texture pop out! Try wearing luxurious accessories like jewelry and use bold makeup. Focus mainly on layering since using only one color would look dull without it.
