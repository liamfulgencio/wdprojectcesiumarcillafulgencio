DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Galactic Glam

Galactic Glam is a theme that basically combines celestial elegance with intergalactic edge. It is inspired by space-like themes like stars, planets, and spage-age futurism. To perfect this theme, try going for a mix of neon-like glowing colors along with black.

For tips, you can try recreating planets or stars with use of patterns or colors of your reference's needing. You can try going for luminous shades of blues and purples along with black to make that space-like theme pop out. You can also go for metallic materials and glitter.
