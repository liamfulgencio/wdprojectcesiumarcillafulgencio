DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Pastel Goth

Pastel Goth is a subtheme of Goth. It basically revolves around blending soft pastel colors with dark hues like black with mystical aesthetics. It mixes gothic elements along with pastels to form an ethereal look.


Some tips for this theme is to go for gothic accessories like skulls, chains, and web patterns. The overall palette of your fit should be a mix of black with pastel colors like pink, lavender, blue, green, or yellow. You may also go for some floral touches like floral crowns. Aside from that, try putting on some fishnet patterns.
