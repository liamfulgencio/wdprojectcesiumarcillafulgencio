DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Decora

Don't know how to do Decora? Here's a guide! Decora is a vibrant Japanese street fashion style focusing on cuteness, playfulness, self-expression, and colorful fits! It is emphasized by the use of bright colors, whimsical and quirky patterns, and accessories. Try going for looks focusing on individuality and joy.


Here are some tips to further improve your look. Try going for neon (but not too neon) colors like pink, green, yellow, and blue. For patterns, you can try focusing on stripes, polka dots, and florals. For accessories, you can go for bows, chunky jewelry, and clips. Never forget to be creative and over-accessorize! Try focusing on layering your clothes.
