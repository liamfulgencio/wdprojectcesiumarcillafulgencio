DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Rococo

Here's a guide for Rococo! This theme embodies the 18th-century French opulence style of clothing. It mainly focuses on intricate details, pastel hues, and playful extravagance. It also centers around a soft-colored theme.


For some styling tips, try going for soft pastel colors and patterns like pale pink, baby blue, beige, or ivory. Try going as well for some details like lace, ruffles, and embroidery. You can also wear extravagant outfits showing off corsets, dresses, hats, fans, gloves, and parasols.
