DRESS TO IMPRESS: A GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

Theme: Koi Fish

Not sure how to do Koi Fish theme? Here's a guide! Inspired by Japanese culture, this theme emobodies the elegant and symbolic features of a koi fish. It is mainly focused on vibrant colors and scales-inspired textures.


Here are some styling tips you can use! Try focuing on vibrant colors mainly centered around orange, red, white, black, and gold. To be more accurate, you can also try using the mermaid tail in the game to be more fish-like. Use accessories that are water-inspired and harmonize vibrant colors with calm, flowing silhouettes.
